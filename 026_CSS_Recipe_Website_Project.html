Gulaab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Prepare Dough: Mix milk powder, flour, and baking soda. Add ghee and knead with milk to form a soft dough. Rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Make Syrup: Boil sugar and water with cardamom or rose water. Simmer until slightly thickened.
  3. Fry Jamuns: Heat oil. Fry small dough balls until golden brown. Drain on paper towels.
  4. Soak in Syrup: Add fried jamuns to warm syrup. Let them soak for at least 30 minutes before serving.
